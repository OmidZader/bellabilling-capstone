# BellaBilling™

### Human-Approved AI Billing Coordinator

Production-oriented AI billing workflow for service businesses.

BellaBilling™ transforms a natural voice conversation into a customer-ready invoice while keeping the business owner in complete control through a human approval workflow.

---

## Overview

BellaBilling™ was developed by ZaderFlo Systems as a production-oriented AI billing coordinator for service businesses.

Rather than replacing the business owner, BellaBilling removes repetitive administrative work by transforming a completed job conversation into a structured invoice draft, routing it through an approval checkpoint, automatically generating a professional PDF invoice, and delivering it to the customer.

This repository accompanies the Google AI Intensive Capstone submission and documents the architecture, workflow, and implementation behind the system.
